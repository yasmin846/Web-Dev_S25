Recipe #2

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 2 min
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 300 gr
Eggs 2 p
Milk 300 ml
Sugar 5 spoons

Steps:

  • In a bowl, mix flour & sugar
  • In another bowl, whisk milk and egg
  • Combine ingredients and stir until smooth
  • Heat a pan and pour to form pancakes.
